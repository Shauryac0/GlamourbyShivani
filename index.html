About Us

Based in Unnao, Glamour By Shivani is a professional bridal makeup artist who has been in the makeup scene since 2020, providing brides across the city and beyond with their dream bridal makeover. She covers everything from bridal makeup to styling and works with the passion to make her clients look and feel like true princesses on their special day. So if you have a wedding coming up and are on the search for a professional makeup artist to do your bridal glam and styling, then she can be a good pick.
